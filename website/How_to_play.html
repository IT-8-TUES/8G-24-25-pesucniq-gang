How to Play

Here's how the game works:

  • Click on the "Quiz" link in the navbar to start the game.
  • You will hear a short audio clip of a song.
  • Type your guess in the answer box.
  • Click "Submit" to check your answer.
  • You’ll get instant feedback — right or wrong!
  • Try to get as many songs right as you can!
